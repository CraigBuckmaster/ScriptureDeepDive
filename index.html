What’s Inside Each Chapter

Hebrew & Greek

Original language word studies with transliterations, roots, and the theological weight each word carries — things translations can’t fully convey.

Historical Context

What was happening in the ancient world when this was written? Who were Israel’s neighbours, what did they believe, and how does this text respond to them?

Deep Commentary

Theological deep dives, interpretive debates across traditions, and the big ideas each passage is wrestling with — from ancient rabbis to modern scholars.

Cross-References

See how each passage connects to the rest of Scripture — how the New Testament picks up Old Testament themes, and how the whole Bible holds together.

Key Places

Detailed cards on every significant location in the text — where it is today, why it mattered, and what was happening there in the ancient world.

Timelines

Visual timelines showing authorship, historical events, and how theological ideas developed across centuries of Scripture and church history.

Tools & References

People of the Bible

An interactive genealogy tree spanning Adam to Paul — ~105 major figures mapped across 8 eras with biographies, family connections, and links to their chapters.

Bible World Map

Explore the ancient biblical world — journeys, regions & key sites on a living parchment map.

Scholar Biographies

Meet the commentators whose voices appear throughout the study notes — from Church Fathers to Reformation theologians to modern scholars.
