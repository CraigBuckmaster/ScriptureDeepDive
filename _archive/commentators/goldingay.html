John Goldingay

BCOT Psalms (3 vols) & Fresh Translation

Biography

John Goldingay is David Allan Hubbard Professor Emeritus of Old Testament at Fuller Theological Seminary. Born in Birmingham, England, he taught at St John’s College, Nottingham before moving to Fuller in 1997.

Key Works

His three-volume Baker Commentary on Psalms (2006\u20132008) offers a fresh translation of every psalm with literary and theological commentary. He has also authored major works on Daniel, Isaiah 40\u201355, and Old Testament theology.

Approach

Goldingay’s approach is literary-theological: he attends to each psalm’s poetic structure, metaphorical language, and emotional register before drawing theological conclusions. His fresh translations often reveal nuances hidden by familiar renderings.
