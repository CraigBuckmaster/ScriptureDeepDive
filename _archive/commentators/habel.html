Norman C. Habel

OTL Job & Courtroom Metaphor

Biography

Norman Habel is an Australian Lutheran theologian and biblical scholar known for pioneering eco-theology and the Earth Bible project.

Key Works

His Old Testament Library commentary on Job (1985) remains a standard academic reference.

Approach

Habel’s key insight is that Job is structured as a legal drama. Job functions as plaintiff, God as defendant, the friends as expert witnesses. The legal vocabulary throughout — witness, arbiter, redeemer, oath, verdict — supports this reading.
