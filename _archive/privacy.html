Privacy Policy

Scripture Deep Dive
Effective Date: March 2026
Developer: Craig Buckmaster

Summary: We collect no personal information. All your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

Scripture Deep Dive does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. No account is required. No email, name, or location data is accessed.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Personal notes you write on verses
  • Bookmarks and verse highlights
  • Reading progress and plan progress
  • Preferences (translation, font size, notifications)

This data is never transmitted to any server and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Analytics

The app may use anonymous crash reporting to improve stability. No personally identifiable information is included.

Push Notifications

Notifications (daily verse, reading plan reminders) are local only — processed entirely on your device.

Third-Party Services

No advertising networks or social media SDKs are integrated. Expo Application Services (EAS) is used for app distribution.

Advertising & Purchases

There are no ads, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

The app collects no personal data from any user, including children.
