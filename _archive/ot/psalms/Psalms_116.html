Psalms 116

I Love the LORD, for He Heard My Voice

Verses 1–9 — “The LORD Is Gracious and Righteous”
123456789

Hebrew Word Study

אָהַבְתִּי ʾāhabtî — I love: "I love (ʾāhabtî) the LORD, for he heard my voice" (v.1). Personal testimony: death’s cords entangled me (v.3), I called on the LORD (v.4), he saved me (v.6). "The LORD is gracious and righteous; our God is full of compassion" (v.5). "Return to your rest, my soul, for the LORD has been good to you" (v.7). The soul that was troubled is counselled: return to rest.

MacArthur Study Notes

MacArthur Study Bible — Faithful Paraphrase
116:1–2

"I love the LORD, for he heard my voice; he heard my cry for mercy. Because he turned his ear to me, I will call on him as long as I live." Love born from being heard. The response to divine attention is lifelong prayer.

116:3–6

"The cords of death entangled me. Then I called on the name of the LORD. The LORD is gracious and righteous. When I was brought low, he saved me." Death’s proximity, God’s rescue, character revealed.

Verses 10–19 — “Precious in the Sight of the LORD Is the Death of His Faithful Servants”
10111213141516171819

Hebrew Word Study

יָקָר yāqār — precious / costly / dear: "Precious (yāqār) in the sight of the LORD is the death of his faithful servants" (v.15). The most-quoted funeral verse. God does not view the death of his faithful casually — it is costly, significant, weighty to him. "What shall I return to the LORD for all his goodness to me? I will lift up the cup of salvation and call on the name of the LORD" (vv.12–13). Paul quotes v.10 in 2 Corinthians 4:13: "I believed; therefore I have spoken."

MacArthur Study Notes

MacArthur Study Bible — Faithful Paraphrase
116:12–13

"What shall I return to the LORD? I will lift up the cup of salvation." The gratitude question: how do I repay God? The answer: receive more. Lift the cup — accept salvation again.

116:15

"Precious in the sight of the LORD is the death of his faithful servants." God does not treat his servants’ deaths lightly. Each one is precious — costly, noticed, mourned.

116:17–19

"I will sacrifice a thank offering to you and call on the name of the LORD, in the courts of the house of the LORD — in your midst, Jerusalem." Public worship: the courts, the city, the community. Testimony is corporate.
