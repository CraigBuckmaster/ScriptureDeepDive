David J.A. Clines

WBC Job & Hebrew Philologist

Biography

David Clines is Emeritus Professor of Biblical Studies at the University of Sheffield, one of the most prolific Old Testament scholars of the late 20th century. He is also editor of the Dictionary of Classical Hebrew (8 volumes).

Key Works

His three-volume WBC Job (1989, 2006, 2011) totals over 2,600 pages — the most comprehensive modern commentary on any biblical book.

Approach

Clines reads Job as sophisticated literature that resists systematic theology. He argues that God never actually answers Job’s question, that the epilogue is deliberately ironic, and that the friends are not simply wrong. His approach is philologically rigorous and theologically provocative.
