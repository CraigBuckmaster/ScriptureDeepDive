Willem A. VanGemeren

EBC Psalms & Reformed OT Scholar

Biography

Willem VanGemeren is Professor Emeritus of Old Testament and Semitic Languages at Trinity Evangelical Divinity School. He edited the five-volume New International Dictionary of Old Testament Theology and Exegesis (NIDOTTE), one of the standard reference works for OT studies.

Key Works

His EBC Psalms (2008) is a comprehensive evangelical commentary that integrates ancient Near Eastern background, Hebrew philology, and Reformed theological reflection.

Approach

VanGemeren reads the Psalms as both historical worship documents and prophetic texts pointing to Christ. He traces royal, messianic, and eschatological themes through the Psalter while attending to each psalm’s literary structure and emotional texture.
