# Release Notes — Scripture Deep Dive

## v1.0.0 (Initial Release)

### Content
- 30 books live: Genesis through 2 Chronicles, Job, Psalms, Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, Song of Solomon, Isaiah, Lamentations, Daniel, Matthew, Mark, Luke, John, Acts, Romans
- 879 chapters of verse-by-verse scholarly analysis
- 43 scholar commentaries across 6 traditions
- 211 people in the genealogy database
- 71 places on the biblical world map
- 28 narrative journey stories
- 216 timeline events
- 45 parallel passage sets
- 15 Hebrew/Greek word studies
- 66 book introductions

### Features
- Dual translation (NIV/ESV) with instant toggle
- Verse Highlight Layer (VHL) — divine names, places, people, time, key terms
- 15+ scholarly panel types per section
- Interactive genealogy tree with pinch-to-zoom and family chaining
- Biblical world map with region polygons and journey polylines
- Proportional timeline with era-colored bands
- Parallel passage comparison with tabbed Gospel views
- Personal notes, bookmarks, and color-coded verse highlights
- 5 reading plans (Gospels, Psalms, Wisdom, Pentateuch, Prophets)
- Text-to-speech with speed control
- Verse sharing as branded image cards
- Daily verse notifications
- Full-text search across verses, people, and word studies
- Entirely offline — no internet required
- Dark theme throughout
