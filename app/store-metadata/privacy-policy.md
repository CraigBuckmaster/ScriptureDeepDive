# Privacy Policy — Scripture Deep Dive

**Effective Date:** March 2026
**Developer:** Craig Buckmaster

## Overview

Scripture Deep Dive is a free Bible study application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects, stores, and shares.

**Summary: We collect no personal information. All your data stays on your device.**

## Data Collection

Scripture Deep Dive does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

- No account creation or login required
- No email addresses, names, or phone numbers collected
- No location data accessed
- No contacts, photos, or files accessed
- No cookies or web tracking
- No advertising identifiers

## Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Personal notes** you write on verses
- **Bookmarks** you create
- **Verse highlights** you apply
- **Reading progress** (which chapters you have visited)
- **Reading plan progress** (which days you have completed)
- **Preferences** (translation choice, font size, notification settings)

This data is **never transmitted** to any server, **never shared** with any third party, and **never accessible** to the developer. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently deleted.

## Analytics

The app may use anonymous, aggregated analytics (such as Expo's built-in crash reporting) to improve stability. These analytics contain:

- No personally identifiable information
- No user-created content (notes, bookmarks, highlights)
- Only technical data: app version, device type, crash stack traces

You may disable analytics in your device's system settings.

## Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications (optional), the app schedules **local notifications only** — such as daily verse reminders and reading plan prompts. These are processed entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

- **Expo Application Services (EAS):** Used for app distribution and over-the-air content updates. Expo's privacy policy applies to their infrastructure services.
- **No advertising networks** are used.
- **No social media SDKs** are integrated.

## Children's Privacy

Scripture Deep Dive does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since the app collects no personal data from any user, no special provisions for children are necessary.

## In-App Purchases and Advertising

There are **no in-app purchases**, **no subscriptions**, and **no advertisements** of any kind.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at the same URL with an updated effective date.
